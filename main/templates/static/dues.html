Camp Dues

Camp dues are $500. Send them to @leddinosaur on Venmo.

We can't guarantee a spot in the camp until dues are paid. That said, we don't want dues to be the reason you can't attend. If the cost is prohibitive for you, talk to Courtney, and we'll find a way to make it work.

It might take a few days after paying dues for it to show up on your Dino profile, so don't worry if it doesn't happen right away!

  • Camp art projects
  • Repair, replacement, storage, and delivery costs for camp equipment
