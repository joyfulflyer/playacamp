Camp Dues

Camp dues are $500. Send them to @leddinosaur on Venmo.

We can't guarantee a spot in the camp until dues are paid. That being said, we don't want dues to be the reason you can't attend. If the cost of dues would be prohibitive for you, talk with Courtney and we'll figure something out.

It might take a few days after paying dues for it to show up on your Dino profile. This is expected, so don't worry if it doesn't happen right away!

  • Booze for the bar
  • LED gifts
  • Camp art projects
  • Repair, replacement, storage, and delivery costs for camp equipment
